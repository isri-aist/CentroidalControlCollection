Detailed Description

Walking control based on tracking of divergent component of motion (DCM)

See the following for a detailed formulation.

  • J Englsberger and C Ott. Integration of vertical CoM motion and angular momentum in an extended capture point tracking controller for bipedal walking. Humanoids, 2012.
  • J Englsberger, et al. Three-dimensional bipedal walking control using divergent component of motion. IROS, 2013.
